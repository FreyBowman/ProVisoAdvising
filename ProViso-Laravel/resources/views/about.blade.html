About Us

Welcome to our online advising platform for computer science!

OUR GOALS

We are a team of experienced computer science professionals dedicated to providing you with high-quality advising services to help you achieve your academic and career goals. Our mission is to provide personalized support to students and professionals in the field of computer science, helping them navigate their way through complex coursework and challenging projects. Whether you need guidance on future classes, want to know about scholarship opportunities, or any other aspect of computer science, our team is here to help. Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, convenient, and accessible, providing you with the flexibility to schedule advising sessions at a time that works best for you. We are committed to providing you with the resources and support you need to succeed in your academic and professional pursuits, and we look forward to working with you!
